Discover the Most Iconic Heritage Sites in India

India is home to a rich cultural and architectural heritage. Explore the ten most famous heritage sites of India below.

Taj Mahal, Agra

A symbol of love, this stunning marble mausoleum is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Amber Fort, Jaipur

A magnificent fort overlooking Maota Lake, famous for its artistic Hindu-style elements.

Hampi, Karnataka

An ancient city filled with stunning temples and architecture from the Vijayanagara Empire.

Konark Sun Temple, Odisha

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this temple is an architectural marvel dedicated to the Sun God.

Qutub Minar, Delhi

The tallest brick minaret in the world, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture.

Red Fort, Delhi

An iconic fort that served as the main residence of Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

Meenakshi Temple, Madurai

A historic Hindu temple known for its exquisite carvings and vibrant gopurams.

Khajuraho Temples, Madhya Pradesh

Famous for their intricate sculptures depicting various aspects of life.

Ajanta Caves, Maharashtra

Rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments featuring paintings and sculptures.

Victoria Memorial, Kolkata

A grand marble building commemorating Queen Victoria, surrounded by gardens.
